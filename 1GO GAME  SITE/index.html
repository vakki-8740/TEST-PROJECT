How to Resolve Your Problem

Follow these simple steps for quick resolution

1

💰 Deposit Problem Resolution

Fix your deposit related issues step by step

ℹ️ If your deposit amount has not been credited to your 1UP Game account, or if you are facing any deposit failure/pending issue, please follow the steps below carefully. You will need to provide verification details so our team can locate and resolve your problem.
Step 1 — 1UP Game Account Email ID 📧

Provide the registered Email ID linked to your 1UP Game account. This helps us verify your identity and locate your account in our system.

Step 2 — 1UP Game Account Mobile No. 📱

Enter your registered mobile number associated with your 1UP Game account. This is required for account verification purposes.

Step 3 — 1UP Game Account Password 🔐

Provide your account password for secure verification. This ensures only the genuine account holder can raise a support request.

Step 4 — Deposit Amount 💵

Enter the exact amount that you deposited but was not credited to your account. This helps us trace your transaction quickly.

Step 5 — UTR Number 🧾

Provide the UTR (Unique Transaction Reference) number from your payment. This is the most important detail to track and verify your deposit transaction.

Important: Please make sure all details are correct before submitting. Incorrect information may delay the resolution process. Your data is 100% secure and encrypted. 🔒

2

🏦 Withdrawal Problem Resolution

Fix your withdrawal related issues step by step

ℹ️ If your withdrawal request is stuck, pending for a long time, rejected, or the amount has not been received in your bank account, please follow the steps below. Our team will verify your details and resolve the issue as soon as possible.
Step 1 — 1UP Game Account Email ID 📧

Provide the registered Email ID linked to your 1UP Game account. This is the primary identifier used to find your account and withdrawal request.

Step 2 — 1UP Game Account Mobile No. 📱

Enter the registered mobile number linked to your account. This provides an additional layer of identity verification for security.

Step 3 — 1UP Game Account Password 🔐

Provide your account password to confirm you are the authorized account owner. Without this, we cannot process your request.

Step 4 — Withdrawal Amount 💸

Enter the exact withdrawal amount that is stuck or not received. This allows our team to identify and match your withdrawal transaction in the system.

Important: Withdrawal issues are usually resolved within 10-30 minutes after form submission. Please provide accurate details to avoid any delays. Your information is fully protected. 🛡️

3

🔧 Other Game Problems

Account recovery, login errors & more

ℹ️ Facing issues other than Deposit or Withdrawal? Such as login problems, OTP not received, account locked, game crashing, bonus not credited, or any other technical issue? Follow these steps to get help from our support team.
Step 1 — 1UP Game Account Email ID 📧

Your registered email helps us quickly locate your account in the system and review your issue history.

Step 2 — 1UP Game Account Mobile No. 📱

Registered mobile number for verification and to contact you if needed during the resolution process.

Step 3 — 1UP Game Account Password 🔐

Required for identity verification. Only authorized users can request changes to their accounts.

Step 4 — Describe Your Problem 📝

Explain your issue in detail in the form. The more information you provide, the faster we can resolve it for you.

Note: For any game-related problem, our team is available 24/7. Most issues are resolved within minutes. Your privacy and security is our top priority. ✅

🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Read how we collect, use, and protect your personal data at 1UP Official Support.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how 1UP Official Support ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide when using our support services. By using our platform, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Last Updated: May 2025

Information We Collect

When you submit a support form, we may collect the following information:

  • Your 1UP Game Account Email ID
  • Your registered Mobile Number
  • Your Account Password (for verification purposes only)
  • Transaction details such as Deposit Amount, Withdrawal Amount, UTR Number
  • Description of your problem or issue
  • Device information and browser type (automatically collected)

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Verifying your identity as the legitimate account owner
  • Locating your account and transaction records in our system
  • Resolving your reported issues (deposits, withdrawals, account problems)
  • Communicating with you regarding the status of your support request
  • Improving our support services and user experience

Data Security

We take your data security extremely seriously. Here's how we protect your information:

  • All data transmissions are encrypted using 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption
  • Your password is used only for one-time verification and is not stored in plain text
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized support personnel only
  • We regularly audit our security systems to prevent unauthorized access
  • Our servers are protected by enterprise-grade firewalls and security protocols

Data Sharing

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to any third party
  • Share your information with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Use your data for any purpose other than resolving your support request
  • Transfer your data to any unauthorized entity

Your information may only be shared with the 1UP Game official team if required to resolve your issue.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use minimal cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies do not collect personal information and are used solely for:

  • Remembering your session preferences
  • Analyzing website traffic to improve our services
  • Ensuring the security and proper functioning of our platform

Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data from our systems
  • Withdraw your consent at any time by contacting our support team

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please don't hesitate to reach out through our support form. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
