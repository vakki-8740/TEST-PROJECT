Privacy Policy

At MELBET Official Support, we take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our support portal.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and your data is secure.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide you with the best support experience:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, mobile number, and game username.
  • Account Information: Login credentials including email and password for account verification.
  • Transaction Details: Deposit amounts, withdrawal amounts, UTR numbers, and payment screenshots.
  • Issue Descriptions: Details about the problems you are facing in the game.
  • Chat Messages: Messages exchanged through our Direct Chat feature.
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, and IP address for security purposes.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity and game account ownership.
  • To process and resolve your deposit and withdrawal issues.
  • To communicate with you regarding your support requests.
  • To improve our support services and user experience.
  • To prevent fraudulent activities and ensure platform security.
  • To send important updates and notifications about your requests.

Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is stored securely using encryption technologies.
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized support team members only.
  • We regularly update our security protocols to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Payment screenshots and sensitive documents are handled with extra care.
  • We do not store your passwords in plain text - they are hashed for security.

Data Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not share your information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law or legal processes.
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes.
  • To trusted service providers who assist us in operating our support portal.

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

As a user of our support portal, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain uses of your data.
  • Right to Portability: You can request transfer of your data.

Cookie Policy

Our support portal uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the portal to function properly.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Security Cookies: Help protect your account and data.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Direct Chat or Submit Form.
