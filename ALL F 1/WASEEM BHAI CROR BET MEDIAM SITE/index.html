FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about deposits, withdrawals, and account issues.

How long does it take to resolve a deposit issue?

+
Most deposit issues are resolved within 2-10 minutes after you submit your problem with the correct details. In rare cases, it may take up to 30 minutes during peak hours. Make sure to provide the correct UTR number for faster resolution.

Why is my withdrawal stuck on pending?

+
Withdrawals can get stuck due to bank processing delays, incorrect bank details, or pending verification. Submit your problem through our support form with your game email, mobile number, password, and the withdrawal amount. Our team will process it manually.

Is it safe to share my password on the support form?

+
Yes, the official CroreBet support form uses secure encrypted submission. Your password is required solely for account verification purposes and is handled by our authorized support team only. Never share your password through any other channel.

What is a UTR number and where do I find it?

+
UTR (Unique Transaction Reference) is a 12-22 digit number assigned to every UPI/bank transaction. You can find it in your payment app (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, etc.) under transaction history. Look for the specific payment you made and the UTR/Reference number will be listed in the transaction details.

My deposit was deducted twice. What should I do?

+
If your account was charged twice, submit a problem with both UTR numbers and the total deducted amount. Our team will verify both transactions and credit the correct amount to your game wallet. Duplicate charges are usually reversed within 15-30 minutes.

Can I submit multiple problems at once?

+
We recommend submitting one problem at a time to ensure accurate and fast resolution. Once your first issue is resolved, you can submit another one. Each submission is tracked separately by our support team.

What if I entered wrong details in the form?

+
If you entered incorrect details, simply submit a new form with the correct information. Our team will process the latest submission. Make sure to double-check your email, mobile number, and UTR number before submitting.
